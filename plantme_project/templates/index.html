Core concept

What your sketch becomes on the website

Plant growth from tasks

Tasks completed by the client generate growth points. One task starts the seed. Ten tasks create a sprout. Twenty tasks unlock a full plant. Forty tasks open a new plant slot.

Therapist-client workflow

Therapists can assign tasks, set frequency, add notes, and review progress. Clients can view work, mark it complete, and send updates or optional proof.

Wellness-triggered rewards

Hydration can water the plant. Outdoor activity can add sunlight. Walking tasks can optionally connect to map-based tracking for proof and motivation.
