The mission

A softer, smarter way to support student mental wellness.

PlantMe is built around one belief: students deserve support before life feels unmanageable.

Problem

Students face ongoing pressure from deadlines, exams, loneliness, financial stress, and major life transitions. Most existing support is either too expensive, too clinical, or too hard to access early.

Solution

PlantMe helps students reduce stress, build coping habits, see burnout patterns early, and step into human support without stigma or pressure.

Core pillars

  • Daily emotional check-ins
  • Habit-based burnout prevention
  • Calming garden growth gamification
  • Support conversations and counseling
  • Real-world wellness events
