The mission

A softer, smarter way to support student mental wellness.

PlantMe is built around one belief: students deserve support before life feels unmanageable.

What this version includes

  • Authentication and user accounts
  • Database-backed check-ins, habits, journals, support requests, and events
  • Rules-based burnout score
  • Map-based event exploration
  • Booking flow tied to logged-in users

  • Email verification and password reset
  • Admin moderation
  • Privacy policy and production deployment
  • Crisis resources and safety escalation
